Chamber Construction

Welcome to the Chamber Construction section. This section manages structural civil works, waterproofing, and mechanical component installations for valve chambers and flowmeter vaults.

Execution Steps

  1. Excavation & Shoring: Clear area and secure trench depths with steel sheets.
  2. PCC Base Blinding: Pouring concrete layer to establish a level workspace.
  3. Waterproofing: Applying external bituminous membranes against water ingress.
  4. Rebar & Formwork: Assembling reinforcement steel cages and rigid wall shutters.
  5. Concrete Pouring & Curing: Structurally casting concrete with proper mechanical vibration.
  6. Mechanical Valve Fitting: Mounting and aligning isolation/air valves and joints.
