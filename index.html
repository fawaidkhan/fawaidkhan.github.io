Construction Activities

Workflow Timeline: Full sequence of site execution tasks tracking operational progression inside the pipeline corridor:

1

Survey & Setting Out

Topographical alignment verification, route staking, and Benchmark establishment along right-of-way.

2

Trial Pits

Physical trial slot excavation to locate and clear existing underground utility systems before works.

3

Excavation

Mechanical trenching down to targeted invert levels according to specific profile design drawing sheets.

4

Shoring

Installation of trench protection walls or frame supports to maintain stability during deep-cut zones.

5

Dewatering

Continuous pumping operations to suppress water table ingress inside deep structural trench runs.

6

Bedding

Spreading and compacting selected sand layers to formulate a unified base foundation cushion support.

7

Pipe Laying

Lowering water main structural barrels into position matching slope and alignment engineering profiles.

8

Welding / Jointing

Execution of specialized structural joint assemblies or weld protocols backed by full NDT checks.

9

Thrust Blocks

Pouring concrete reaction mass constraints at pipeline change-of-direction bends and branches.

10

Backfilling

Selected surround sand cover packing and layered soil structural compaction with Warning Tape installation.

11

Testing

Conducting segmented hydrostatic pressure integrity verifications against strict project metrics.

12

Chlorination

Chemical line sterilization, scrubbing flushes, and water sampling to acquire hygiene clearance certificates.

13

Commissioning

Controlled valved system pressure charge equalization, live telemetry startup tests, and official handover.
