About Pipeline Engineering

Pipeline engineering involves the specialized design, construction, operation, and maintenance of pipeline systems. These systems form the critical infrastructure required to safely transport liquids, gases, and slurries over long distances.

Core Expertise & Services

  • Hydraulic Analysis: Flow modeling, pressure drop calculations, and surge analysis.
  • Mechanical Design: Pipe stress analysis, wall thickness calculation, and material selection.
  • Integrity Management: Corrosion control, inline inspection data analysis, and risk assessments.
